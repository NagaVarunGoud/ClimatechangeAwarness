Global Warming

Reduce greenhouse gas emissions by using renewable energy sources, increasing energy efficiency, and supporting policies that limit carbon emissions.

Rising Sea Levels

Implement coastal protection measures, reduce emissions to slow ice melting, and prepare for coastal flooding through community planning.

Extreme Weather Events

Invest in resilient infrastructure, improve early warning systems, and support disaster preparedness and response initiatives.

Loss of Biodiversity

Deforestation

Support reforestation projects, reduce paper consumption, and promote sustainable agriculture and logging practices.

Melting Glaciers

Reduce greenhouse gas emissions and support research and conservation efforts to slow the melting of glaciers and ice caps.

Disruption of Food Systems

Promote sustainable agriculture, support local food systems, and reduce food waste to adapt to changing climate conditions.

Impact on Human Health

Improve public health infrastructure, promote climate-resilient health systems, and address health risks associated with climate change.

Economic Costs

Invest in climate adaptation and mitigation measures to reduce the financial impact of climate change on communities and economies.
